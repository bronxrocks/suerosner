The Rock and Roll Hall of Fame may be located in Cleveland, but the Bronx is well represented. Dion DiMucci was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 1989. "Wall of Sound" record producer Phil Spector was inducted the same year.

Bobby Darin was inducted in 1990.

While the Chantels have not yet been inducted, they were nominated for the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 1999 and again in 2010. In 2002, they were inducted into the Vocal Group Hall of Fame located in Sharon, Pennsylvania. That same year, they were inducted into the Bronx Walk of Fame.

Also inducted into the Vocal Group Hall of Fame are The Chiffons. They were inducted in 2005.
